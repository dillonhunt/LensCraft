üîß Troubleshooting

Make sure you've granted camera permission. Go to Settings > LensCraft > Camera and ensure it's enabled. If the issue persists, try closing and reopening the app, or restart your device.
The flashlight may not work if the camera is actively recording video in another app, or if the device is too hot. Close other camera apps and let your device cool down if needed.
To save photos, you need: (1) LensCraft Pro, and (2) Photo Library permission. Go to Settings > LensCraft > Photos and select "Add Photos Only" or "Full Access".
Tap "Restore Purchase" in the app's Settings under LensCraft Pro. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. If you continue to have issues, contact us at support@emberlabs.app.
